The unstructured data paradox

A real business is messy. GST is rigid. Nobody bridges the gap.

📥

Invoices arrive everywhere

WhatsApp photos, printed bills, thermal receipts, scanned PDFs — scattered across 30 days and dozens of supplier chats.

🕳️

GSTR-2B can't see its own gaps

If a supplier never files, the entry simply doesn't exist. The portal has no way to flag what's missing — only the trader's own invoices can.

🔒

The portal won't explain

It's English-only, built for filing, not diagnosis. It never says why ₹2,400 is stuck or what to do about it.

What's inside

Built around one test: can a kirana owner act on this alone?

Missing-transaction detection

Diffs your invoices against GSTR-2B to catch suppliers who never filed — the loss the portal can't show you.

HSN, rate & PoS mismatch

Line-item matching surfaces wrong HSN codes, tax rate differences, and Place of Supply errors that quietly block credit.

Section 17(5) blocked credit

Flags invoices for catering, vehicles, or club memberships where ITC is blocked by law — before you try to claim it.

WhatsApp bot

Forward an invoice photo to the bot, get an instant ITC verdict. Built on Meta's Cloud API with webhook signature verification.

Ask a CA — AI chat

Type any GST question in Hindi, English, or Marathi. Powered by Gemini with a local Qwen3 4B fallback that works offline.

3 languages via Sarvam AI

Full UI in Hindi, English, and Marathi. Speech-to-text input and real-time translation for AI responses — powered by Sarvam.

IMS walkthrough

Step-by-step guide to Accept, Reject, or Hold on the GST portal. The app recommends — you file. Never automatic.

One-tap supplier message

Generates a polite WhatsApp message to the supplier with the exact issue and fix needed. Hindi and English.

Export & share

Download reconciliation reports as CSV, PDF, or share as text. Works on web and native — cross-platform export.
